Frequently Asked Questions

What CFexpress Type B transfer speeds does the ProGrade PG05.5 achieve?

Real transfer speeds are 850-1100 MB/s depending on the card and port. Theoretical maximum is 1250 MB/s. Requires a true USB 3.2 Gen 2 port (not Hub-limited connections) to achieve near-max speeds.

Can I transfer CFexpress and SD cards simultaneously at full speed?

Yes. The ProGrade PG05.5 has dual slots with independent controllers, allowing simultaneous offload of CFexpress Type B and SD UHS-II cards without speed degradation.

Can I hot-swap cards without losing data?

Yes, hot-swapping is safe. The reader supports live card insertion and removal without powering down. Always wait for transfer completion and safe-eject confirmation before removing cards.

Does the ProGrade work with Canon R5, Nikon Z9, and Sony A1?

Works with Canon R5/R6/R7 (CFexpress + SD), Nikon Z9 (CFexpress), Sony A1 (CFexpress Type A—requires Sony MRW-G2). Check your camera's card slot specifications for compatibility.
