How to Update Your Car's Navigation

  1. Find your part number: Check the label on your existing navigation SD card.
  2. Select your vehicle: Click on your car model above.
  3. Choose a compatible card: Review options and compare features.
  4. Install: Turn off engine, locate SD slot, swap cards, restart vehicle.
