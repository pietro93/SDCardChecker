Installation Guide for Your GMC Models

1. Turn off vehicle and remove key. 2. Locate SD slot, typically found inside the center console armrest storage compartment. 3. Press existing card to eject. 4. Insert new card firmly with contacts facing down until it clicks. 5. Start vehicle - the IntelliLink system will auto-detect and begin loading new maps in 1-2 minutes.

Important: GMC Models Compatibility

Verify the part number on your existing card is critical for GMC models. Compatible with IntelliLink navigation systems (IO5, IO6, IO8) only. Not for base radio systems.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which navigation system does this GMC SD card update?
This map update is designed specifically for 2014-2023 GMC vehicles equipped with the 8-inch IntelliLink (IO5, IO6, or IO8) factory embedded navigation system. Always verify your specific system via the part number.
My GMC center console has multiple slots; which one do I use?
If your GMC's center console features a media hub with various ports, insert the card strictly into the slot designated for 'SD Card' or with a small SD card icon. Be careful not to force it into USB ports located directly adjacent to it.
Do I need to go to a GMC dealer to activate the new map card?
No dealer visit is necessary. The navigation cards are completely plug-and-play. Just insert the card into your GMC's SD slot, and the IntelliLink system will authenticate and begin loading the new map data automatically.
Will this update enhance the navigation in my GMC Acadia or Terrain?
Yes, for 2014-2023 GMC Acadia and Terrain models equipped with the factory IntelliLink navigation system, this SD card will provide the latest road network and Points of Interest updates.
