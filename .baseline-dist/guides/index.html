Common Questions

What speed class do I need?

For 4K video: V30 or U3. For burst photography: U1-U3. For everyday use: U1. See detailed guide →

How much storage do I need?

Depends on your use: 1000 JPEG photos ≈ 5GB, 1000 RAW photos ≈ 30GB, 1 hour 4K video ≈ 150GB. Use our calculator →

RAW or JPEG?

JPEG for instant sharing and storage efficiency. RAW for editing control and archival. Professionals often use both. See comparison →

What about H.264 vs H.265?

H.265 is more efficient (50% smaller files). H.264 is more compatible (older devices, streaming). See comparison →
