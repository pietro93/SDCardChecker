## Mobile/Responsive Behavior

### Desktop (>768px)
- Featured image: max 400px, centered
- Alternatives: 3-column grid
- Table: Full width with scroll if needed
- Sidebar: Visible

### Tablet (768px)

- Sidebar: May collapse

### Mobile (<480px)
