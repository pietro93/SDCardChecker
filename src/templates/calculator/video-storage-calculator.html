Frequently Asked Questions

H.264: The industry standard for video compression. Good quality at manageable file sizes. Compatible with almost all devices.

H.265 (HEVC): Newer codec with better compression. Files are 40-50% smaller than H.264 at the same quality. Some older devices may not support it.

ProRes: Professional mezzanine codec. Much larger files but superior quality for color grading and post-production. Used in professional workflows.

4K 30fps: V30 minimum. Write speed of 30 MB/s ensures consistent recording without dropped frames.

4K 60fps: V60 recommended. Higher frame rate requires faster sustained writes.

8K or professional: V90 required. Highest performance for demanding workflows.

Check your manual: Most cameras specify bitrate for each recording mode.
