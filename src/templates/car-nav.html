Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between navigation SD cards?

Navigation cards are pre-loaded with map data for specific vehicle models and years. Different manufacturers (Navtimi, ORIRI·X, etc.) may offer slightly different coverage areas or pricing. All are plug-and-play compatible with factory navigation systems.

How do I know which part number I need?

Check the label on your existing navigation card (usually found in your vehicle's SD card slot). You'll see a part number like "86271-0E077" or "85713512". Match that number when ordering your replacement.

Will 2025 maps work on older model years?

Yes! The navigation system reads the card data regardless of the map year. 2025 maps have the latest road data and improvements. However, verify your vehicle's factory navigation is compatible with the specific part number.

Can I update my vehicle's navigation system myself?

Yes! It's simple: turn off the engine, locate the SD card slot in your vehicle (usually in the center console or below the screen), eject the old card, insert the new one, and restart. No technical knowledge required.
