How to Choose an SD Card Reader

Interface Type: USB-C for modern devices, USB-A for broader compatibility, CFexpress for professional workflows.

Speed: UHS-I (104 MB/s) for general use, UHS-II (312 MB/s) for professional video/photography.

Slots: Single-slot readers are portable, multi-slot readers handle multiple card types simultaneously.

Compatibility: Check your device's ports and the card types you use (SD, microSD, CFexpress, etc.).
